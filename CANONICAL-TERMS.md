# ALETHEIA19 Canonical Ontology
This document provides the normative, zero-entropy definitions for the core structural invariants of the ALETHEIA19 framework and the FASCIA auditing protocol.

---

## Facade Homeostasis
* **Canonical Identifier:** `facade-homeostasis`
* **Status:** Stable
* **First Appearance:** FASCIA v1.0
* **Definition:** The autonomic preservation of organizational appearance, narrative coherence, and outward structural representations through hollow consensus. It masks underlying metabolic deficits or operational entropy by consuming adaptive reserves to maintain surface stability.

---

## Semantic Parasitism
* **Canonical Identifier:** `semantic-parasitism`
* **Status:** Stable
* **First Appearance:** FASCIA v1.0
* **Definition:** The systemic drift where an organization's operational vocabulary, concepts, and labels are captured and repurposed by administrative or marketing superstructures (R=0). This decouples the vocabulary from actual physical constraints (R=1) and leads to the generation of design chimeras.

---

## Semantic Oxygen Debt
* **Canonical Identifier:** `semantic-oxygen-debt`
* **Status:** Stable
* **First Appearance:** FASCIA v1.0
* **Dependencies:** `facade-homeostasis`, `semantic-parasitism`
* **Definition:** The structural accumulation of narrative complexity and statistical abstraction exceeding the system's metabolic capacity to maintain coherent, biophysical coupling with real-world physical constraints (R=1). It represents the thermodynamic gap between operational narrative and physical reality.

---

## Temporal Extraction
* **Canonical Identifier:** `temporal-extraction`
* **Status:** Stable
* **First Appearance:** FASCIA v1.0
* **Definition:** The kinetic acceleration of organizational processes and iteration loops designed to capture immediate financial or statistical yields. This acceleration forces the premature closure of adaptive fallow cycles, exhausting the systemic resource substrate.

---

## Chiral Mirroring
* **Canonical Identifier:** `chiral-mirroring`
* **Status:** Stable
* **First Appearance:** FASCIA v1.0
* **Definition:** The symmetric inversion of structural patterns and operational signals when projected across the boundary between the physical substrate (Sol, R=1) and the statistical representation layer (Codex, R=0). Under chiral mirroring, an optimized metric in the Codex often represents a deficit in the Sol.
